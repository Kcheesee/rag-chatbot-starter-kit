# Acme Returns & Warranty Policy

## Eligibility

Items are eligible for return within 30 days of delivery, provided they are unused
and in original packaging. Final-sale and personalized items cannot be returned.

## How to start a return

1. Visit your order history and select **Return**.
2. Print the prepaid return label.
3. Drop the package at any carrier location within 14 days.

## Warranty

All Acme products carry a **one-year limited warranty** covering manufacturing
defects. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse or normal wear. To make a
warranty claim, contact support with your order number and a description of the issue.

## Refund timing

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed to the original
payment method within five business days. Bank posting times may add a few days.
