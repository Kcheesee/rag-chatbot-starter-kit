# Acme Support FAQ

A small example knowledge base so you can verify the pipeline end to end before
connecting your own documents.

## What is the refund window?

You can return most items within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund. Items
must be unused and in their original packaging. Refunds are issued to the original
payment method within five business days of us receiving the return.

## How long does shipping take?

Standard orders ship within **2 business days**. You'll receive tracking by email as
soon as your order leaves the warehouse. Expedited shipping is available at checkout.

## Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to most countries. International delivery typically takes 7–14 business
days, and any customs duties are the responsibility of the recipient.

## How do I contact support?

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9am–6pm ET. Email
support@acme.example or use the in-app chat for the fastest response.
