Acme Corporation — Company Overview

Acme Corporation was founded in 2010 and provides widgets and gadgets to customers
worldwide. The company is headquartered in Columbus, Ohio, and operates fulfillment
centers in Ohio, Nevada, and the Netherlands.

Acme's mission is to make high-quality everyday hardware affordable and reliable.
The company employs roughly 400 people across engineering, operations, and support.

Business hours for customer support are Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm Eastern
Time. Acme observes all US federal holidays.
